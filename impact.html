Future Effects and Possible Responses

  1. Future Effects
    • Extreme weather such as intense hot extremes, heavy precipitation, droughts, and tropical cyclones
    • Global surface temperature will continue to increase. Global warming will exceed 1.5 degrees Celsius within decades without interventions
    • Sea level is expected to rise 1 to 8 feet by 2100
    • Intensity, frequency, and duration of hurricanes are projected to increase
    • Ocean and land will be less effective at absorbing and slowing the accumulation of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere
  2. Possible Responses
    • Shift from burning of fossil fuels to renewable energy such as solar panels
    • Include less meat and dairy products in diet
    • Consume less items and purchase eco-friendly items if possible
    • Switch to electric vehicle to reduce greenhouse gas emissions
    • Share knowledge and increase awareness related to climate change
